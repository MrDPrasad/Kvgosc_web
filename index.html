Kvgce open-source community

A community driven by tech enthusiasts and open-source contributors, to help young students be part of the open-source ecosystem by providing training and skill development.

Get Trained by Experts

KVGOSC is all about helping each other learn and improve. We conduct workshops and training sessions led by experts from other technical communities and student experts. Stay updated with the latest technologies and learn new skills.

Explore Clubs and Activities

KVGOSC is a community of clubs and individuals who contribute to our events and activities. Explore our active clubs like Mozilla Campus Clubs, Developer Students Clubs, and GitHub Campus Experts.

Get Connected!

Meet like-minded people, collaborate on projects, and host events to foster a better collaborative environment. We are over 100 members with diverse interests in technology, always eager to connect and collaborate.

Join community

KVGOSC is a dynamic community of tech enthusiasts dedicated to learning, collaboration, and innovation. Join us to explore exciting projects, grow your skills, and connect with like-minded individuals. Write to us to become part of the community!
