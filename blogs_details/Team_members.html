The Visionaries Behind Our Open Source Community

Our college’s Open Source Community is led by a dedicated team, including President Nithish Vasanth, Vice President Durga Prasad N, and other key members who manage technical projects, events, finances, and marketing. Together, they create an inclusive space for students to learn, collaborate, and make impactful contributions. With their guidance, the community is set to drive innovation and growth in open-source development.

Details

Our college’s Open-Source Community is driven by a passionate and dedicated team, each bringing unique skills and a commitment to fostering innovation and collaboration. With a vision to make open-source development accessible and impactful, our team works tirelessly to create an inclusive space for students to learn, build, and grow together.

President: Nithish Vasanth

As President, Nithish Vasanth leads our community with a focus on innovation, inclusivity, and student empowerment. He oversees the community’s operations, ensuring that our goals align with students' aspirations. Nithish’s leadership inspires members to reach their full potential and drives the team to maintain a high standard of excellence.

Vice President: Durga Prasad N

Durga Prasad N supports the President in all aspects, bringing strong organizational skills and strategic insights to the community. As Vice President, he is dedicated to creating a supportive environment for members and ensuring that everyone has opportunities to learn and contribute.

Secretary: Manikanta S

Manikanta S plays a crucial role as Secretary, handling the documentation, communication, and scheduling necessary for the community to function smoothly. His organizational skills and attention to detail keep the team on track and ensure that all events and projects run seamlessly.

Technical Leads: Keerthan V and Nithish Kumar M S

Our Technical Leads, Keerthan V and Nithish Kumar M S, guide the community’s projects with expertise and enthusiasm. They mentor members in various technical domains, from software development to AI and web technologies, fostering a collaborative learning environment where everyone can enhance their skills.

Treasurer: Ajay P

Ajay P manages the community’s finances as Treasurer, ensuring that funds are used effectively to support our initiatives and events. His meticulous budgeting and planning allow the team to pursue projects and host workshops.

Marketing Lead: Ashith K E

Ashith K E, our Marketing Lead, is responsible for promoting the community’s activities and engaging with a broader audience. His creativity and communication skills ensure that our events reach as many students as possible.

Event Lead: Anvith S U

As Event Lead, Anvith S U organizes workshops, hackathons, and meetups that bring our community together. His passion for event planning and collaboration helps create experiences that are both educational and enjoyable.
